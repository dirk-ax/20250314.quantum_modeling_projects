Program Overview

The Axiomatic_AI Education Initiative on Truthful and Verifiable AI seeks to establish a new paradigm for AI education in quantum science, focusing on mathematical consistency and formal verification of AI outputs.

This program is designed to address the growing need for reliable AI systems in scientific research and education, particularly in quantum computing and related fields.

Key Objectives

1. Curriculum Development

Create comprehensive educational materials on quantum computing with mathematical verification at every step.

2. Tool Creation

Develop open-source software tools that demonstrate Axiomatic_AI's approach to verification in quantum contexts.

3. Research Promotion

Support research initiatives that advance the integration of formal methods with AI in quantum science.

Program Scope

This initiative covers various aspects of quantum science education, including:

  • Quantum computing fundamentals
  • Quantum algorithms and complexity theory
  • Quantum error correction and fault tolerance
  • Quantum simulation techniques
  • Quantum machine learning approaches
  • Verification methods for quantum systems

Application Process

Eligibility Criteria

  • Advanced degree in quantum physics, computer science, or related field
  • Demonstrated interest in formal verification methods
  • Research proposal aligning with program objectives

How to Apply

Submissions are accepted on a rolling basis. To apply, please prepare:

  1. CV/Resume with relevant experience and publications
  2. Research proposal (max 5 pages)
  3. Two letters of recommendation

Send all materials to education@axiomatic-ai.org with the subject "Education Initiative Application"
